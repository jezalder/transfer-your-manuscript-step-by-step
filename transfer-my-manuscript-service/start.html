More information about this service
Why use this service?
Using this service takes much of the hard work out of resubmitting. Just choose your journal and transfer, and we'll move your submission to your chosen journal.
You can still make changes to your submission before approval, and can cancel at any time. Please note your submission will still be subject to the normal peer review process.
What happens next?
The corresponding author will receive an email to confirm the transfer. After confirmation we will send the manuscript to the selected journal.
The journal will invite the corresponding author to sign in and continue with the submission.
The authors can make any desired changes before approving the PDF, and they can cancel at any time.
Peer-review in your new journal
The peer-rerview process in your new journal remains the same. Your paper will still need to go through the peer-review process
